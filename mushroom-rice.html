Mushroom-Rice

Description

Mushroom-Rice is a quick easy one pot meal.
It is a savoury rice dish that comes together with minimum fuss in a pressure-cooker.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups Jeera rice or Basmati rice soaked for 10 minutes and drained aside.
  • 200 gms button mushrooms cooked till firm ,water drained and measured to use.
  • Hot boiling water+mushroom cooked water = 2 1/2 cups
  • ginger, garlic paste 1teaspoon
  • whole spices cinnamon 1 inch piece
  • cloves 3,cardamom 3
  • 2,3 Bay leaves
  • 8,10 whole pepper corns
  • 1/2 tabsp Ghee
  • 1/2 tabsp Coconut oil

Steps

  1. Add the ghee and oil to a pressure cooker(min 3litre)and lightly saute the whole spices
    till you get a nice aroma.
  2. Saute the ginger ,garlic paste and add the drained rice to this.
    Stir fry over high flame for 5-7 minutes till almost dry.
  3. Pour the measured hot water,sufficent salt and close the cooker with the weight on.
    Cook on high for 1 whistle
  4. When steam settles,add the cooked mushrooms and close lid
    Let it simmer without weight for 5 minutes
  5. Open the lid and fluff up the rice gently with a fork.
    Serve it hot on a dish with a few mushrooms as garnish.

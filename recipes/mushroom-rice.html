"Mushroom-Rice"

"Description" Mushroom-Rice is a savoury rice that can be had as a one pot meal or with any side of choice.

Jeera rice a small variety of rice is lightly sauted with ginger,garlic and a few whole spices

Its cooked with double volume water on high flame in a pressure cooker for 1 whistle.After the pressure settles

cooked button mushrooms are added as the last step and allowed to simmer for 5 minutes in the pressure cooker without the weight.

Once opened and fluffed up with a fork,enjoy this tasty,aromatic dish.

Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups jeera rice soaked for 10 minutes and drained.
  • boiling hot water+ mushroom cooked water=3 cups
  • 1/2 tablespoon ghee
  • 1/2 tablespoon coconut oil
  • whole spices cinnamon 1 inch piece
  • 3 cloves
  • 2,3 Bay leaves
  • 3 cardamom
  • 8,10 whole pepper corns
  • 200gms button mushrooms cooked,drained water measured

Steps
  1. Add the ghee and oil to a pressure cooker and lightly saute the whole spices,Bay leaves.

  2. Add the drained rice to this and stir fry over high flame for 5-7 minutes till almost dry.

  3. Pour the measured hot water,sufficent salt and close the cooker.Cook on high for 1 whistle.

  4. When steam settles,add the cooked mushrooms and close lid.let it simmer for 5minutes.

  5. Open the lid and fluff the rice gently with a fork.Serve it hot on a plate with a few mushrooms as garnish.

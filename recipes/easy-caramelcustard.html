Easy-CaramelCustard

Description

Caramel Custard is a simple favorite dessert.This easy recipe ensures a creamy slab like texture with no eggy taste.A slice of powdered bread compensates for an egg in the recipe with no compromise on taste.The end result is a perfect caramelized flan that can be either steamed or baked in a water-bath.

Ingredients

  • Full-fat milk 2cups
  • Eggs 3
  • Thin bread slice 1
  • Vanilla essence 1/2 teaspoon
  • Sugar 10 teaspoon
  • Sugar 2 1/2 tablespoon for caramelizing

Steps

  1. Caramalize 2 1/2 tablespoon sugar in a compact steel or pyrex dish

  2. Blitz the bread slice in mini jar of mixi.

  3. Beat the 3 eggs.Add rest of the ingredients.Beat with a whisk till well blended.

  4. Pour the mixture over the caramalized sugar.Cover with lid and steam for 15,20 minutes till done.

    5. Or bake in a water-bath at 175 degrees C(350 degrees F) till done.

  5. When slightly cool,run a knife all round and demold onto a rimmed platter.

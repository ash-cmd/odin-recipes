Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish which is almost as popular as the favorite pizza.It can be considered as a one pot meal with all the deliciousness of pasta,ground meat,tomato sauce,herbs,blends of cheeses specially the ubiquitous mozarella cheese which come together in a perfect medley.

.

The Lasagna pasta noodle is cooked al dente and layered with all of the ingredients in succession.This continues till all of the pasta,meatIN which when all baked to a golden brown makes for a mouthful of yummy goodness.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles

  • 1 pound lean ground beef

  • salt and pepper to taste

  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce

  • 1 clove garlic minced

  • 1/2 pound shredded mozarella cheeese

  • 1/2 pound shredded cheddar cheese

  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.

    Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente.Drain.

  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.(175 degreesC)

    In a large skillet over medium high heat,brown beef and season with salt and pepper;drain.

    Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.

  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well.

    In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.

  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
